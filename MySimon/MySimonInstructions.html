Classic Game Play Summary:

Re-PETE begins by offering one tone/color that the player must match. Upon a successful match, one tone/color is added to the sequence. And so on. There are ten possible levels to proceed through. Completing level 10 is a WIN!

Classic Game Play Detail:
  • Press New Game to start.
  • Match the tone/color offered by Re-PETE.
  • If correct, Re-PETE will replay the last tone/color sequence and add another tone/color sequence
  • Play proceeds until round ends.
    • Failure to press a tone/color within 3 seconds.
    • An incorrect sequence.
    • Matching the entire sequence (25 tones/colors)
  • Press Again and Re-PETE will start next round.
  • A level consists of 3 rounds (sequences). The player must obtain 15 matches in those 3 rounds to move past level 1. Each succeeding level requires a higher number of matches (the level threshold).
  • If the level threshold is not met, the level is repeated.
  • The game is over when the player completes level 10.
